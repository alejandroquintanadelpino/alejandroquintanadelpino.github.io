Blackpink Mac10 & Lightstick
---
New 3D project after some time away! I've been working on something special for me, a Blackpink-themed weapon and a model of their iconic lightstick, which also works as a keychain for the weapon. This project is particularly exciting for me as it's the first time I've used Vray, and I'm very pleased with the results.
---
Maya, Substance 3D Painter, Photoshop, DaVinci Resolve, Vray
